These two graduate courses trace a single thread: how engineering data becomes trustworthy evidence, and how that evidence flows to the right person, in the right format, at the right time.

Drawing on 20 years of AEC practice and ongoing research in semantic digital twins, both courses combine case-based instruction with a term-long capstone project — students leave with a governance architecture or integration plan they can defend, not just a grade.

## Courses

- **[Engineering Data Governance for Sustainable Strategy](engineering-data-governance)** — 工程資料治理永續策略 · Fall · 3 Credits
- **[Smart Construction Flow Integration Practice](smart-construction-flow-integration)** — 智慧營建流程整合實務 · Spring · 3 Credits
