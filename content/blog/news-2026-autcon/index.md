Our paper **"Semantic Digital Thread Framework for Explainable Lifecycle Carbon Management: An Ontology-Driven and Auditable Automation Backbone"** has been published in *Automation in Construction* (Vol. 188, 107026).

The paper proposes an ontology-driven semantic framework that automates lifecycle carbon data collection and traceability across building phases, supporting explainable and auditable ESG reporting.
